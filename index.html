About Me

Hi, I’m Elena Polyakova, a dedicated, results-driven full stack web developer based in London, ON. with a strong passion for leveraging technology to enhance user experiences.

I graduated in 2022 with Advanced Diploma from Mobile Applications Development at St. Clair College in Windsor, Ontario, where I learned how to design and develop web, desktop and mobile apps. During my studies, I completed several courses and projects on web development, such as Web Design and Web Application Development.

My expertise includes more than 4 years of experience in designing, coding, testing, implementing, and creating responsive websites using a wide range of tools and technologies.

If you are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic web developer to work on your website, please feel free to connect with me. I look forward to discussing how I can help you achieve your web development goals.
