About Me

Hi, I’m Elena Polyakova, a web developer based in London, ON. I build engaging and user-friendly websites for clients who want to establish or enhance their online presence. I use my technical skills and creativity to design and develop websites that meet the specific needs and goals of each client. Whether you need a simple landing page, a blog, an e-commerce site, or a complex web application, I can help you bring your vision to life on the internet.

I graduated in 2022, with an Advanced Diploma in Mobile Applications Development from St. Clair College in Windsor, Ontario, where I learned how to design and develop web, desktop and mobile apps. During my studies, I completed several courses and projects on web development, such as the Web Design and Web Application Development.

If you are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic web developer, to work with you one on one on your website or in a team, please feel free to contact me. I look forward to discussing how I can help you achieve your web development goals.
