About Me

Hi, I’m Elena Polyakova, a web developer based in London, ON. I build engaging and user-friendly websites for clients who want to establish or enhance their online presence. I use my technical skills and creativity to design and develop websites that meet the specific needs and goals of each client. Whether you need a simple landing page, a blog, or an e-commerce site, I can help you bring your vision to life on the internet.

I graduated in 2022, with an Advanced Diploma in Mobile Applications Development from St. Clair College in Windsor, Ontario, where I learned how to design and develop web, desktop and mobile apps. During my studies, I completed several courses and projects on web development, such as the Web Design, UI/UX Design, and Web Application Development.

I work online, for clients all over Canada, with the skills and tools to meet your needs and vision. I can use HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, React, Laravel,WordPress, PHP, and JavaScript to create responsive, user-friendly, and accessible websites. I may use tools like Google Meet and Zoom to communicate and collaborate with my clients and share my work.

If you are looking for a reliable and enthusiastic web developer for your project, please reach out to me. I would love to hear about your web development needs and how I can help you achieve them.
